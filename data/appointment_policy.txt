APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING POLICY
Springfield General Hospital - Patient Services Department

Scheduling an Appointment
Patients may schedule appointments through the following methods: online via the patient portal at the hospital website, by phone at 555-000-1234 Monday through Friday 8 AM to 5 PM, in person at the registration desk at any clinic location, or through the Springfield General mobile app. New patients must provide valid photo identification, insurance card, and referral if required by their insurance plan at the time of their appointment.

Appointment Confirmation
All appointments are confirmed via text message or email 48 hours in advance. Patients must confirm their appointment by responding to the confirmation message. Unconfirmed appointments may be released to other patients on the waiting list.

Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy
Patients who need to cancel or reschedule must notify the hospital at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations can be made through the patient portal, mobile app, or by calling the scheduling line at 555-000-1234. Late cancellations with less than 24 hours notice or no-shows may result in a $25 administrative fee for established patients. Urgent appointments may be requested by calling the main scheduling line or through the nurse advice line.

Arriving for Your Appointment
Patients should arrive 15 minutes before their scheduled appointment to complete any necessary paperwork. Late arrivals beyond 10 minutes may need to be rescheduled at the discretion of the clinical staff.

What to Bring
Patients should bring government-issued photo identification, insurance card and co-pay, a list of current medications, completed intake forms available online, and referral documents if applicable.

Pediatric Appointments
A parent or legal guardian must accompany patients under 18 years of age. Guardians must present legal documentation if they are not the biological parent.

Interpreter Services
Free interpreter services are available for patients who require language assistance. Please notify scheduling staff when booking your appointment so arrangements can be made in advance.

After-Hours Urgent Care
For urgent medical concerns after office hours, patients may visit our 24-hour urgent care center at 100 Health Way Springfield or contact the nurse advice line at 555-000-2000.

Available Departments
The hospital offers appointments in the following departments: cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, pediatrics, dermatology, general medicine, oncology, psychiatry, and radiology. Specialist appointments may require a referral from a primary care physician depending on insurance requirements.
